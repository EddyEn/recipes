Ingredients
- Meats: can be chicken,beef or seafood.
- Basil leafs
- Oyster sauce
- chilli
- garlic
- veg: can be blocolli, carot, and/or mashroom.
- sugar
- chicken stock
- Fish sauce

1. Fry shopped chilli and garlic in a hot pan until the garlic turns gold color.
2. Add meat to the hot pan and stir until the meat is half cooked.
3. Add veggiesthen stir until the veg is cooked. (if the veggi is blocolli, it shoul be boiled first.)
4. Add oyster sauce, fish sauce, sugar, chicken stock, then stir until all are mixed.
5. Add basil leaf, stir for a few second then turn the fire burnner off.
